Since post-spike price of cryptocurrencies show some consistent patterns, it would make more sense to use the post-spike prices in the prediction models. Two models (ARIMA and LSTM) were developed for price prediction. It was found that ARIMA model predicted better than LSTM model in this project's context, which is also apparent when the predecitions are plotted.

Currencies of USDT (USD) market in Bittrex.com can be entered below to test the models described above. Once executed the tool provides the current price information, predicted price in a table format and the plots of the past and predicted price.
